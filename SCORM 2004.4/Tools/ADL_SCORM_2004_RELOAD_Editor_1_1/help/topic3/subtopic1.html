Funding
RELOAD is part of the X4L (Exchange For Learning) program funded by the Joint Information Systems Committee (JISC). The JISC promotes the innovative application and use of information systems and information technology in further and higher education across the UK. More details about X4L are available from http://www.jisc.ac.uk/index.cfm?name=programme_x4l

Duration
RELOAD is funded for 18 months, from November 2002 to April 2004.
