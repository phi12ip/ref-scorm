RELOAD+VDEX
The IMS VDEX (Vocabulary Definition Exchange) draft specification (http://www.imsglobal.org/vdex/), released in September (final release expected 15 November 2003) covers the mapping of vocabularies between different domains to facilitate interoperability of metadata records. At Heriot-Watt University, Phil Barker and Tina Manoharan have started work on creating a VDEX editor built on top of RELOAD. Whilst by no means a finished product, anyone interested in this specification should visit their web page to find out more and download the latest build. The tools, and further information is available from: http://www.icbl.hw.ac.uk/vdex/
